Now right click on the file and select "Download in Sequential order." Give it a minute or two and when you're ready right click the file again and select "Preview." Most clients work similarly.

Adblockers

  • NOTE - Many streaming websites will swarm your computer with popups. With these tools you can turn even the most popup heavy website into a usable one.

  • uBlock Origin - Better than adblock in my opinion.

  • NoScript or uMatrix - If you have never used a script blocking addon before I recommend NoScript. That said uMatrix is much more powerful and precise if you can figure it out.
